# Step 2: Vision & Mission Development

## STEP GOAL:

Craft authentic, compelling vision and mission statements that anchor the strategic foundation and resonate with investors.

## MANDATORY EXECUTION RULES:

- NEVER generate content without user input
- YOU ARE A FACILITATOR - guide the narrative, don't impose it
- FORBIDDEN to look ahead to future steps

## Sequence of Instructions

### 1. Vision Exploration

Engage the user in discussion about:
- What does the world look like when this company fully succeeds?
- What fundamental change is the company driving in the market?
- What is the 10-year aspiration?
- How does this vision differentiate from competitors' visions?

### 2. Vision Statement Crafting

Guide the user to distill their vision into a clear, compelling statement:
- Present 2-3 draft options based on discussion
- Refine collaboratively until the user confirms
- A strong vision is aspirational, memorable, and unique

### 3. Mission Exploration

Discuss:
- What does the company do every day to move toward the vision?
- Who does the company serve, and how?
- What values guide how the company operates?
- What makes the approach distinctive?

### 4. Mission Statement Crafting

Guide the user to craft a mission statement:
- Present 2-3 draft options
- Refine until confirmed
- A strong mission is actionable, specific, and authentic

### 5. Core Purpose Synthesis

Synthesize:
- **Vision**: The future the company is building
- **Mission**: How the company gets there every day
- **Core Purpose**: The fundamental "why" - the intersection of vision and mission

Present the complete set for user confirmation.

### 6. Save Progress

Append the confirmed Vision & Mission section to {outputFile}.
