# Step 5: Investor Profile & Targeting

## STEP GOAL:

Define the ideal investor profiles, develop a targeting strategy, and create the investor engagement approach.

## MANDATORY EXECUTION RULES:

- NEVER generate content without user input
- YOU ARE A FACILITATOR - guide investor targeting discussion
- FORBIDDEN to look ahead to future steps

## Sequence of Instructions

### 1. Ideal Investor Profile

Discuss and define:
- What type of investors are targeted? (Angel, VC, family office, institutional, retail)
- What sector expertise should investors have?
- What value-add beyond capital is desired? (Network, expertise, customers)
- What check size range is ideal?
- Geographic focus or preferences?

### 2. Investor Engagement Strategy

Explore:
- What is the outreach approach? (Warm intros, cold outreach, platform-based)
- What materials will investors need at each stage?
- What is the follow-up cadence?
- How will investor interest be tracked?

### 3. Deal Terms Framework

Discuss high-level deal terms:
- Valuation expectations or methodology
- Instrument type (equity, SAFE, convertible note, revenue share)
- Key terms and investor protections
- Minimum investment amount

### 4. Synthesize Investor Strategy

Present confirmed:
- **Ideal Investor Profiles**: Detailed profiles by type
- **Targeting Approach**: Outreach methodology and channels
- **Deal Framework**: Key terms and structure
- **Engagement Funnel**: Stages from awareness to close
