# Step 5: Brand Guidelines Completion

## STEP GOAL:

Finalize the brand guidelines with usage examples, application guidance, and executive summary.

## Sequence of Instructions

### 1. Usage Guidelines

Define application standards:

**Application Examples:**
- Business card layout
- Email signature format
- Letterhead design
- Presentation template guidance
- Investor deck template guidance
- Data room document formatting

**Co-Branding Rules:**
- Partner logo placement
- Minimum brand presence requirements
- Approval process for co-branded materials

**Digital Usage:**
- Social media profile standards
- Website header/footer standards
- Email template standards
- Digital advertising guidelines

**Print Usage:**
- Document printing standards
- Large format and signage guidelines
- Material and finish preferences

**Misuse Examples:**
- Common brand violations to avoid
- Corrective examples showing proper usage

### 2. Generate Executive Summary

Based on all gathered content, draft an executive summary:
- Brand positioning overview
- Visual identity scope
- Voice and tone foundation
- Usage guidelines coverage
- Brand consistency assessment

Present the draft summary to the user for review and refinement.

### 3. Recommended Next Steps

Based on the brand guidelines, recommend:
- **Investor Narrative** to align messaging with brand voice
- **Design Investor Platform** to apply visual identity
- **Data Room** to apply document formatting standards
- Brand asset creation (logo files, templates, etc.)
