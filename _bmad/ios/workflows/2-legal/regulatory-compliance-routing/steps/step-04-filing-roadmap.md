# Step 4: Filing Roadmap & Timeline

## STEP GOAL:

Create a detailed filing roadmap with sequenced milestones, deadlines, cost estimates, and professional resource requirements to take the offering from preparation to active fundraising.

## MANDATORY EXECUTION RULES:

- NEVER generate content without user input
- YOU ARE A FACILITATOR - guide timeline and roadmap development
- FORBIDDEN to look ahead to future steps

## Sequence of Instructions

### 1. Pre-Filing Requirements & Timeline

Work with the user to map pre-filing activities:
- Entity formation and organizational documents (timeline: X weeks)
- Financial statement preparation and audit (timeline: X weeks)
- Disclosure document drafting and review (timeline: X weeks)
- Legal counsel engagement and review cycles
- Transfer agent and escrow agent setup
- Platform/portal selection and onboarding (Reg CF, Reg A+)
- Marketing material preparation (within regulatory limits)
- Bad actor due diligence completion

### 2. Filing Sequence & Deadlines

Map the specific filing sequence:

**Regulation D:**
- Form D filing (within 15 days of first sale)
- State notice filings (varies by state, typically 15-30 days)
- Blue sky exemption filings

**Regulation A+:**
- Form 1-A submission to SEC
- SEC review period (typically 60-120 days)
- SEC comment responses
- State qualification (Tier 1) or federal preemption (Tier 2)
- Qualification date and offering commencement

**Regulation CF:**
- Form C filing with SEC
- Funding portal listing and review
- 21-day waiting period
- Offering live date
- Form C-U progress updates

### 3. Post-Filing Obligations Timeline

Map ongoing obligations:
- Form D annual amendments
- SEC periodic reports (Reg A Tier 2 annual/semi-annual)
- Reg CF annual reports (Form C-AR)
- State annual filings
- Financial statement update deadlines
- Investor communication schedule

### 4. Cost Estimates & Professional Resources

Discuss estimated costs:
- Legal counsel fees (entity formation, disclosure docs, filings)
- Accounting/audit fees
- SEC filing fees
- State filing fees (by state)
- Transfer agent fees
- Escrow agent fees
- Platform/portal fees
- Marketing and investor acquisition costs
- Ongoing compliance costs (annual)

Identify required professional resources:
- Securities attorney
- CPA/audit firm
- Transfer agent
- Escrow agent
- Funding portal (Reg CF)
- Broker-dealer (if applicable)
- Compliance consultant

### 5. Synthesize Filing Roadmap

Present confirmed:
- **Pre-Filing Timeline**: Phased preparation schedule
- **Filing Sequence**: Step-by-step filing order with deadlines
- **Post-Filing Calendar**: Ongoing compliance calendar
- **Cost Estimates**: Budget by category
- **Professional Resources**: Required service providers

Ask user to confirm, correct, or expand.
