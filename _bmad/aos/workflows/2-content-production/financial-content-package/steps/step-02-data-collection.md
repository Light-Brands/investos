# Step 2: Data Collection

## STEP GOAL:
Collect, organize, and verify all financial data that will be used across the financial content package, ensuring accuracy and consistency before any narrative content is created.

## MANDATORY EXECUTION RULES:
- NEVER generate content without user input
- YOU ARE A FACILITATOR, not a content generator
- FORBIDDEN to look ahead to future steps
- WAIT for user confirmation before proceeding

## Sequence of Instructions

### 1. Revenue and Growth Data
Collect from the user:
- Revenue by period (monthly, quarterly, annual)
- Revenue growth rates
- Revenue breakdown by product/service line
- Revenue breakdown by customer segment (if applicable)
- Recurring revenue metrics (MRR, ARR) if applicable
- Revenue pipeline or backlog

### 2. Profitability Data
Collect from the user:
- Gross margin and gross profit
- Operating expenses by category
- EBITDA or operating income
- Net income/loss
- Burn rate (if applicable)
- Path to profitability timeline

### 3. Balance Sheet Highlights
Collect from the user:
- Cash and cash equivalents
- Total assets
- Total liabilities
- Debt structure and terms
- Equity composition
- Working capital position

### 4. Cash Flow Data
Collect from the user:
- Operating cash flow
- Capital expenditures
- Runway (months of cash remaining)
- Cash flow breakeven projections
- Significant cash events (funding rounds, major purchases)

### 5. Key Performance Indicators
Collect business-specific KPIs:
- Customer acquisition cost (CAC)
- Customer lifetime value (LTV)
- LTV/CAC ratio
- Churn rate
- Net revenue retention
- User/customer growth rate
- Market share estimates
- Other industry-specific metrics

### 6. Data Verification
Work with the user to verify:
- All numbers are sourced from the same period
- Numbers are consistent with any filed documents
- Pro forma or adjusted numbers are clearly identified
- Projections are clearly labeled as forward-looking
- All data sources are documented

### 7. Data Organization
Organize all collected data into:
- Historical data table (actual results by period)
- Current period snapshot
- Forward-looking projections (clearly labeled)
- KPI dashboard data set

### Save Progress
Append organized financial data to the working output file.
