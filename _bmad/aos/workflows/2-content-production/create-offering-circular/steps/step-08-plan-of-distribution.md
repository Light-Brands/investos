# Step 8: Plan of Distribution

## STEP GOAL:
Describe how the offering will be conducted, including distribution channels, intermediaries, compensation arrangements, and the mechanics of how investors subscribe.

## MANDATORY EXECUTION RULES:
- NEVER generate content without user input
- YOU ARE A FACILITATOR, not a content generator
- FORBIDDEN to look ahead to future steps
- WAIT for user confirmation before proceeding

## Sequence of Instructions

### 1. Distribution Method
Work with the user to describe:
- Is this a direct offering, through a broker-dealer, or through a platform?
- Who is the intermediary (funding portal, broker-dealer, or none)?
- Is there an underwriter? If so, firm commitment or best efforts?
- Will the offering be conducted online, in-person, or both?

### 2. Offering Mechanics
Detail how the offering works:
- How do investors subscribe (online platform, subscription agreement)?
- What is the subscription process step-by-step?
- Is there a minimum closing amount (escrow conditions)?
- What happens if the minimum is not reached?
- Will there be multiple closings or a single close?
- How are funds held during the offering period (escrow)?

### 3. Compensation and Fees
Disclose all compensation arrangements:
- Broker-dealer or platform commissions and fees
- Finder's fees or referral fees
- Success fees or milestone payments
- Warrants or equity compensation to intermediaries
- Any other costs borne by investors or the issuer

### 4. Marketing and Solicitation
Describe the marketing approach:
- General solicitation methods (if 506(c) or Reg A/CF)
- Online marketing channels
- Investor events or roadshows
- Media and PR activities
- Social media approach
- Marketing materials to be used

### 5. Investor Qualification
Detail the qualification process:
- How will investor accreditation be verified (if required)?
- What suitability standards apply?
- Investment limits for non-accredited investors
- Self-certification requirements
- KYC/AML procedures

### 6. Closing Procedures
Describe the closing process:
- Conditions to closing
- How securities will be issued (book entry, certificates, blockchain)
- When investors gain ownership
- Post-closing notifications and confirmations
