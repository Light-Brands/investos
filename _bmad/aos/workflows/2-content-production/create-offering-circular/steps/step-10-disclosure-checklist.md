# Step 10: Disclosure Checklist

## STEP GOAL:
Complete a comprehensive disclosure checklist to verify that all required disclosures are present, properly placed, and adequately detailed in the offering document.

## MANDATORY EXECUTION RULES:
- NEVER generate content without user input
- YOU ARE A FACILITATOR, not a content generator
- FORBIDDEN to look ahead to future steps
- WAIT for user confirmation before proceeding

## Sequence of Instructions

### 1. Required Disclosures by Regulation
Walk through the regulation-specific disclosure requirements:

**For Reg A (Form 1-A / Offering Circular)**
- [ ] Cover page with required information
- [ ] Summary of the offering
- [ ] Risk factors
- [ ] Dilution
- [ ] Plan of distribution
- [ ] Use of proceeds
- [ ] Business description
- [ ] Management discussion and analysis
- [ ] Directors, officers, and significant employees
- [ ] Compensation
- [ ] Interest of management in certain transactions
- [ ] Securities being offered
- [ ] Financial statements (audited for Tier 2)

**For Reg CF (Form C)**
- [ ] Offering terms
- [ ] Company information
- [ ] Risk factors
- [ ] Use of proceeds
- [ ] Business description
- [ ] Financial condition
- [ ] Officers, directors, and ownership
- [ ] Financial statements
- [ ] Related party transactions

**For Reg D (PPM)**
- [ ] Cover page with legends
- [ ] Summary of terms
- [ ] Risk factors
- [ ] Business description
- [ ] Use of proceeds
- [ ] Management
- [ ] Financial information
- [ ] Subscription procedures

### 2. Cross-Section Consistency Check
Verify consistency across sections:
- Do financial figures match across all sections?
- Are company details consistent throughout?
- Do dates and timelines align?
- Are there contradictions between sections?

### 3. Legend and Disclaimer Verification
Confirm all required legends are present:
- [ ] SEC qualification/exemption legend
- [ ] Forward-looking statements disclaimer
- [ ] No guarantee of returns disclosure
- [ ] Restricted securities notice
- [ ] State-specific legends (if applicable)
- [ ] Broker-dealer or platform disclosures

### 4. Gap Analysis
Identify any gaps:
- What required information is still missing?
- What sections need additional detail?
- What information must come from legal counsel?
- What information requires third-party input?

### 5. Checklist Sign-Off
Present the complete checklist to the user:
- Review each item and mark as complete, incomplete, or N/A
- Document any items requiring legal counsel input
- Note any items that need additional user input

### Save Progress
Append the completed disclosure checklist to the working output file.
