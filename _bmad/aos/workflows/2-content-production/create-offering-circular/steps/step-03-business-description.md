# Step 3: Business Description

## STEP GOAL:
Draft the business description section, providing investors with a thorough understanding of the company, its operations, market, competitive position, and growth strategy.

## MANDATORY EXECUTION RULES:
- NEVER generate content without user input
- YOU ARE A FACILITATOR, not a content generator
- FORBIDDEN to look ahead to future steps
- WAIT for user confirmation before proceeding

## Sequence of Instructions

### 1. Company Overview
Work with the user to describe:
- Company history and founding story
- Mission and vision statements
- Current stage of development (concept, startup, growth, established)
- Legal structure and key subsidiaries (if any)
- Principal offices and operations locations

### 2. Products and Services
Detail with the user:
- Core products or services offered
- Revenue model and pricing strategy
- Stage of development for each product/service
- Intellectual property (patents, trademarks, trade secrets)
- Technology platform or key assets
- Product roadmap and development pipeline

### 3. Market and Industry
Describe the market with the user:
- Total addressable market (TAM) and serviceable market (SAM)
- Industry overview and key dynamics
- Market growth drivers and projections
- Regulatory environment affecting the market
- Reference market intelligence report if available

### 4. Competitive Position
Work with the user to present:
- Competitive landscape overview
- Key competitors and their market positions
- Competitive advantages and differentiation
- Barriers to entry
- Market share (current or targeted)

### 5. Business Strategy and Growth Plan
Document with the user:
- Current business strategy
- Growth strategy and key milestones
- Customer acquisition strategy
- Partnership and distribution strategy
- Timeline for key strategic objectives

### 6. Employees and Operations
Gather from the user:
- Number of employees (full-time, part-time, contractors)
- Key operational infrastructure
- Supply chain or vendor dependencies
- Insurance and risk management

### Save Progress
Append the business description section to the working output file.
