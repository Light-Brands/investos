# Step 6: Management Team

## STEP GOAL:
Present the management team, board of directors/advisors, and governance structure in a way that builds investor confidence while meeting disclosure requirements.

## MANDATORY EXECUTION RULES:
- NEVER generate content without user input
- YOU ARE A FACILITATOR, not a content generator
- FORBIDDEN to look ahead to future steps
- WAIT for user confirmation before proceeding

## Sequence of Instructions

### 1. Executive Officers
For each executive officer, gather from the user:
- Full legal name and age
- Title and role
- Professional biography (relevant experience, education)
- Date appointed to current position
- Time devoted to the company (full-time, part-time, percentage)
- Compensation (salary, equity, bonuses -- as required by regulation)
- Other business activities and commitments

### 2. Board of Directors / Managers
For each board member, gather:
- Full legal name and age
- Position on the board
- Professional biography
- Independence status (independent, insider)
- Committee memberships
- Compensation for board service
- Relationship to the company and management

### 3. Advisory Board (if applicable)
For any advisors, gather:
- Name and professional background
- Advisory role and expertise area
- Compensation arrangement
- Relationship to management

### 4. Governance Structure
Work with the user to document:
- Board structure and meeting frequency
- Key committees (audit, compensation, governance)
- Voting structure and control
- Related party transactions and conflicts of interest
- Indemnification and D&O insurance

### 5. Key Person Risk Disclosure
Address key person dependencies:
- Which individuals are critical to the business?
- What happens if key persons leave?
- Are there non-compete or non-solicitation agreements?
- Is there key person insurance?

### 6. Bad Actor Disclosure
Confirm with the user (as required by regulation):
- Have any covered persons been subject to disqualifying events?
- Previous securities violations, orders, or enforcement actions
- Criminal convictions related to securities or financial matters
- If yes, appropriate disclosure must be included
