# Step 9: Legal Matters

## STEP GOAL:
Draft the legal matters section covering the terms of the securities, material agreements, legal proceedings, taxation, and other legal disclosures required for the offering document.

## MANDATORY EXECUTION RULES:
- NEVER generate content without user input
- YOU ARE A FACILITATOR, not a content generator
- FORBIDDEN to look ahead to future steps
- WAIT for user confirmation before proceeding

## Sequence of Instructions

### 1. Description of Securities
Work with the user to detail the terms:
- Class and type of securities being offered
- Par value (if applicable)
- Voting rights (including protective provisions)
- Dividend or distribution rights and preferences
- Liquidation preferences
- Anti-dilution protections
- Conversion rights (if convertible securities)
- Redemption rights
- Preemptive rights or rights of first refusal
- Tag-along and drag-along provisions

### 2. Material Agreements
Identify and summarize with the user:
- Operating agreement or bylaws provisions relevant to investors
- Subscription agreement terms
- Shareholder agreement or investors' rights agreement
- Intellectual property assignments
- Key commercial contracts
- Loan agreements or debt instruments
- Employment agreements with key persons
- Real estate leases

### 3. Legal Proceedings
Disclose with the user:
- Any pending or threatened litigation
- Regulatory proceedings or investigations
- Historical legal matters that may be material
- Claims or disputes that could affect the business
- If none: appropriate "no material legal proceedings" statement

### 4. Tax Considerations
Provide overview of tax considerations:
- Federal income tax implications for investors
- State and local tax considerations
- Entity-level taxation (pass-through vs. corporate)
- Tax reporting obligations for investors
- Recommendation to consult individual tax advisor

### 5. Transfer Restrictions
Detail the restrictions on securities:
- Holding period requirements
- Resale restrictions under applicable regulations
- Right of first refusal (if any)
- Transfer approval requirements
- Legend requirements on certificates or book entries

### 6. Additional Legal Disclosures
Cover any remaining required disclosures:
- Where the offering document can be obtained
- How to contact the issuer with questions
- Availability of additional information
- Integration clause and entire agreement references

### Save Progress
Append the legal matters section to the working output file.
