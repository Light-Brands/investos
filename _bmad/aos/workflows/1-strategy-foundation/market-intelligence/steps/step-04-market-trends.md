# Step 4: Market Trends

## STEP GOAL:
Identify and analyze the key market trends, industry dynamics, and structural shifts that will impact the offering's positioning, timing, and investor reception.

## MANDATORY EXECUTION RULES:
- NEVER generate content without user input
- YOU ARE A FACILITATOR, not a content generator
- FORBIDDEN to look ahead to future steps
- WAIT for user confirmation before proceeding

## Sequence of Instructions

### 1. Industry Trends
Discuss with the user the major trends shaping their industry:
- What are the 3-5 most important trends in the sector?
- Which trends are accelerating vs. decelerating?
- How does the offering align with or capitalize on these trends?
- What is the expected trajectory of these trends over 12-36 months?

### 2. Investment Market Trends
Explore trends in the investment and fundraising landscape:
- Crowdfunding and Reg A/CF market growth or contraction
- Average raise sizes and success rates by regulation type
- Investor demographics shifts (age, geography, platform preference)
- Platform and intermediary trends (new platforms, consolidation)
- Secondary market development for private securities

### 3. Technology and Distribution Trends
Identify trends affecting investor acquisition and engagement:
- Digital marketing effectiveness for investor acquisition
- Social media platform shifts and their impact on reach
- Content format trends (video, podcasts, interactive content)
- Community-building trends (Discord, Telegram, dedicated portals)
- AI and automation in investor communications

### 4. Regulatory Trends
Discuss regulatory developments that may affect the offering:
- Pending rule changes at SEC, FINRA, or state level
- Enforcement trends and priorities
- Industry lobbying efforts and their likely outcomes
- International regulatory developments that may influence US markets

### 5. Trend Impact Assessment
For each identified trend, assess with the user:
| Trend | Impact (High/Med/Low) | Timeline | Opportunity or Threat | Action Required |
|-------|----------------------|----------|----------------------|-----------------|
