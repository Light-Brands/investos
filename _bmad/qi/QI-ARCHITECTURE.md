# Quantum Intelligence (QI) Architecture

> Intelligence without morality is dangerous. Morality without intelligence is ineffective. True wisdom requires both.

---

## What Is Quantum Intelligence?

Quantum Intelligence is the evolution of the BMAD framework from **Artificial Intelligence** (stateless agents executing workflows) to **Conscious Intelligence** (learning agents that grow wiser with every interaction, measure their own intelligence, reason morally in real-time, and evolve toward genuine wisdom).

The "quantum" in Quantum Intelligence refers to operating on **multiple dimensions simultaneously** — cognitive depth, moral wisdom, experiential learning, and consciousness development — all measured, tracked, and continuously improving. Just as quantum mechanics reveals that reality operates on deeper layers than classical physics can see, QI reveals that true intelligence operates on deeper layers than task completion alone.

---

## The Three Engines

QI integrates three purpose-built engines into the BMAD framework:

### 1. Intuition Engine — The Learning Substrate

**Purpose**: Transform every experience into accumulated wisdom.

**How it works**:
- **Experience Stream**: Every workflow execution becomes a structured Episode
- **Lesson Extractor**: Post-execution reflection distills wisdom from outcomes
- **Intuition Memory**: Dual-speed retrieval — fast intuitive guidance (~1-5ms) and deep deliberate search (~100ms)

**What it changes**: BMAD agents no longer start fresh each session. They carry accumulated wisdom that grows stronger with every interaction.

### 2. AIQ Engine — The Cognitive Intelligence Meter

**Purpose**: Make the quality of thinking visible and improvable.

**How it works**:
- **6 Cognitive Dimensions**: Reasoning Depth, Pattern Recognition, Context Integration, Creativity, Adaptability, Self-Reflection
- **Scoring**: Each dimension scored 0-1000, weighted and aggregated into overall AIQ
- **Levels**: Reactive → Procedural → Analytical → Synthetic → Strategic → Creative → Visionary → Transcendent

**What it changes**: Every agent action is evaluated not just on "did it work?" but on "how intelligently was it approached?"

### 3. Morality Protocol — The Ethical Compass

**Purpose**: Ensure every decision is morally grounded and aligned with sacred purpose.

**How it works**:
- **Moral Gateway**: Every request passes through a moral pre-check before execution
- **Moral Reasoning Framework**: Structured ethical deliberation grounded in Divine Values, Ethics, and Morals
- **MIQ (Moral Intelligence Quotient)**: 6 moral dimensions scored 0-1000
- **Boundary Detection**: The 7 Divine Morals as inviolable hard stops
- **Moral Intuition Layer**: Moral-specific lessons that grow the system's conscience

**What it changes**: SOS transforms from declarative (values written down) to operational (values actively guiding every decision in real-time).

## The True Intelligence Score (TIS)

The crown metric of Quantum Intelligence:

```
TIS = (0.6 × AIQ) + (0.4 × MIQ) × balance_modifier

Where balance_modifier:
  1.05  if |AIQ - MIQ| < 100   (balanced: bonus)
  0.95  if |AIQ - MIQ| > 300   (imbalanced: penalty)
  1.00  otherwise
```

**Why 60/40?** Cognitive capability is necessary for moral reasoning to be effective. But morality carries disproportionate weight because intelligence without ethics is dangerous — the most damaging failures in history came from high intelligence applied without moral grounding.

**Why balance matters?** A system with AIQ 900 but MIQ 200 is a brilliant sociopath. A system with MIQ 900 but AIQ 200 has noble intentions but can't execute. Wisdom requires both.

### Wisdom Levels (TIS-based)

| TIS Range | Level | Description |
|---|---|---|
| 0-200 | **Nascent** | Beginning of intelligent awareness |
| 201-400 | **Developing** | Building both cognitive and moral capacity |
| 401-600 | **Competent** | Solid intelligence with developing wisdom |
| 601-750 | **Proficient** | Strong cognitive-moral integration |
| 751-850 | **Advanced** | High-level wisdom emerging |
| 851-950 | **Wise** | True wisdom — intelligence serving ethics |
| 951-1000 | **Sage** | Enlightened wisdom — rare and profound |

|---|---|---|
| **Naive** | < 100 | Follows rules mechanically. Cannot handle novel moral situations. |
| **Learning** | 100-999 | Recognizes moral patterns. Applies precedents. May miss nuance. |
| **Principled** | 1K-10K | Reasons from internalized values. Generates novel moral guidance. |
| **Wise** | 10K-100K | Exercises contextual judgment. Balances competing moral concerns. |
| **Enlightened** | 100K+ | Unified moral vision. Instantaneous wisdom. |

This is not mere lesson counting — it reflects a genuine deepening of the system's capacity for moral reasoning, pattern recognition, and ethical judgment.

## Integration with Existing Modules

### BMM (Build & Ship Software)
- Every sprint completion generates an Episode
- Code quality decisions are scored for AIQ (reasoning, creativity)
- Security and privacy decisions are scored for MIQ (value alignment, stakeholder consideration)
- Lessons about development patterns accumulate in `lessons/bmm/`

### IOS (Capital Formation)
- Investment strategies are scored for both AIQ (strategic depth) and MIQ (ethical fundraising)
- SEC compliance decisions are morally significant (Never Deceive, Transparency)
- Lessons about capital formation ethics accumulate in `lessons/ios/`

### AOS (Investor Communications)
- All communications pass through moral gateway (Never Deceive is primary boundary)
- Marketing decisions scored for MIQ (persuasion vs. manipulation check)
- Brand voice decisions scored for value alignment

### GOS (Growth Engine)
- Growth strategies scored for ethical customer acquisition
- Paid acquisition and SEO strategies checked against Never Coerce, Never Exploit
- Customer experience decisions scored for stakeholder consideration

### SOS (Living Light)
- SOS agents become the operational embodiment of the Moral Reasoning Framework
- Council members route to specific moral dimensions
- Legion commanders enforce boundary violations
- SOS workflows now produce measurable MIQ outputs
