# IOS Compliance Standards

Securities compliance standards and regulatory references for InvestOS capital formation workflows.

## Key Regulatory Frameworks

### Regulation D
- **506(b)**: Up to 35 non-accredited investors, no general solicitation, unlimited raise
- **506(c)**: Accredited investors only (must verify), general solicitation permitted, unlimited raise
- Form D filing required within 15 days of first sale

### Regulation A+
- **Tier 1**: Up to $20M/12 months, state blue sky required, no ongoing reporting
- **Tier 2**: Up to $75M/12 months, federal preemption, annual/semi-annual reporting required
- SEC qualification required for both tiers

### Regulation CF
- Up to $5M/12 months
- Must use FINRA-registered funding portal
- Investment limits for non-accredited investors
- Annual reporting required

## Document Standards

### PPM Requirements
- Business description with material detail
- Risk factors (comprehensive and specific to the offering)
- Use of proceeds with allocation percentages
- Management team backgrounds with relevant experience
- Financial statements (audited if Reg A+ Tier 2)
- Terms of the offering
- Subscription procedures

### Required Disclaimers
- "This is not an offer to sell" on marketing materials
- Past performance disclaimer on any historical data
- Forward-looking statement safe harbor
- Investment risk acknowledgment

## Review Standards
- All investor-facing documents require compliance review before distribution
- Financial claims must be substantiated and sourced
- Cross-reference validation required across all data room documents
- Annual update of all ongoing disclosure documents
