# AOS Campaign Standards

Standards for investor communication campaign planning, execution, and measurement.

## Campaign Architecture

### Campaign Types
- **Awareness**: Broad reach, brand building, thought leadership
- **Nurture**: Relationship building, education, trust development
- **Conversion**: Direct investment solicitation (compliance-critical)
- **Retention**: Post-investment communication, updates, engagement
- **Crisis**: Rapid response communications

### Channel Strategy
- Email: Primary channel for direct investor communication
- Portal: Central hub for document access and updates
- Social: Awareness and thought leadership
- Webinar/Video: Education and engagement
- In-Person: Roadshows and one-on-one meetings

## Campaign Metrics

### Key Performance Indicators
- Email: Open rate, click rate, reply rate, unsubscribe rate
- Portal: Logins, document views, time on site
- Social: Engagement rate, follower growth, click-through
- Conversion: Expression of interest, qualification rate, investment rate
- Overall: Pipeline value, conversion velocity, cost per acquisition

### Benchmarks (Investor Communications)
- Email open rate: 25-40% (higher for existing investors)
- Email click rate: 3-8%
- Portal engagement: 2+ sessions/month for active prospects
- Social engagement: 2-5% engagement rate

## Quality Gates
- All campaign content must pass compliance review before launch
- A/B testing recommended for email subject lines and CTAs
- Weekly performance review during active campaigns
- Post-campaign retrospective required for all major campaigns
