# AOS Brand Voice Standards

Brand voice and visual identity standards for AmplifyOS investor communications.

## Voice Attributes

### Core Voice Characteristics
- **Trustworthy**: Build confidence through transparency and accuracy
- **Professional**: Institutional-grade quality in every communication
- **Clear**: Complex concepts made accessible without oversimplification
- **Confident**: Assertive without being aggressive or promissory

### Tone Matrix by Context
- **Offering Documents**: Formal, precise, legally careful
- **Investor Deck**: Confident, compelling, data-supported
- **Email Campaigns**: Professional but warm, relationship-building
- **Social Media**: Accessible, engaging, still compliant
- **Crisis Communications**: Calm, authoritative, transparent
- **Investor Updates**: Balanced, forthcoming, forward-looking

## Writing Guidelines

### Do
- Use active voice
- Lead with the most important information
- Support claims with specific data
- Include proper context for all metrics
- Maintain consistent terminology across all materials

### Don't
- Use promissory language ("guaranteed", "will achieve", "certain to")
- Oversimplify complex risks
- Use jargon without explanation (unless audience is sophisticated)
- Make comparisons without proper context
- Use superlatives without substantiation ("best", "only", "first")

## Visual Identity Standards
- Consistent color palette across all materials
- Typography hierarchy for readability
- Data visualization guidelines (chart types, labeling)
- Logo usage and clear space requirements
- Photography/imagery guidelines
