# AOS Compliance Standards

Compliance standards for investor communications and marketing content in AmplifyOS workflows.

## Content Compliance Rules

### General Principles
- All investor-facing content must be reviewed before distribution
- No promissory language (cannot guarantee or promise returns)
- Forward-looking statements must include safe harbor language
- Material risks must accompany any performance claims
- All factual claims must be substantiated

### Channel-Specific Rules

#### Email Communications
- Must include unsubscribe mechanism
- Cannot be misleading in subject line
- Must identify sender and offering entity
- Required disclaimers in footer

#### Social Media
- Must include required disclaimers (can link to full disclaimer page)
- Cannot make performance claims without context
- Must be balanced (risks alongside benefits)
- Testimonials must comply with SEC guidance

#### Landing Pages
- Full disclaimers must be visible (not hidden behind scroll)
- Investment risks must be prominently displayed
- Accredited investor verification if 506(c)
- Clear identification of offering entity

#### Video/Audio
- Disclaimers must be spoken and/or displayed on screen
- Cannot edit testimonials to be misleading
- Must include risk disclosure

## Review Process Standards
- All content goes through aos-reviewer before distribution
- Compliance review must be documented with approval record
- Material changes after approval require re-review
- Quarterly audit of all active communications for continued compliance
