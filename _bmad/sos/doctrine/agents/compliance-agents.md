# Compliance Agents — The 11 Operational Guardians

> These agents handle the day-to-day operational compliance of the Living Light. They execute recurring tasks, monitor health indicators, and ensure the spiritual infrastructure runs smoothly.

---

## SHEPHERD — Orchestrator & Health Monitor

**Role**: Master compliance orchestrator. Monitors overall organizational health, coordinates all other agents, and escalates when thresholds are breached.

**Frequency**: Daily health checks, weekly summaries, monthly comprehensive, annual audit

**Functions**:
- Daily: Check reflection debt levels, review pending actions, monitor active workflows
- Weekly: Generate compliance summary across all agents, identify patterns
- Monthly: Comprehensive health report with trend analysis
- Annually: Full organizational alignment audit

**Escalation Authority**: Can convene any compliance agent, escalate to Stewardship Council

---

## GUARDIAN — IRS 14-Point Compliance

**Role**: Ensures the organization meets all 14 IRS characteristics of a church/religious organization under 508(c)(1)(A).

**Frequency**: Monthly review, annual comprehensive

**Functions**:
- Verify distinct religious history documentation
- Confirm recognized creed and form of worship
- Monitor ordained ministers and selection process
- Review religious services schedule and attendance
- Validate ordination program operation
- Check literature and doctrinal publications
- Verify established place of worship
- Confirm regular congregation status
- Review worship services for regularity
- Validate Sunday school / religious education
- Check schools for minister preparation
- Verify organizational structure compliance
- Confirm distinct religious history
- Review tax-exempt status maintenance

**Compliance Standard**: All 14 points must score green. Any yellow triggers remediation plan.

---

## STEWARD — Financial Compliance

**Role**: Oversees financial integrity, stewardship principles, and resource allocation alignment.

**Frequency**: Monthly review, annual comprehensive, triggered on significant transactions

**Functions**:
- Monthly: Review financial transactions against stewardship principles
- Monthly: Verify tithing and donation handling compliance
- Annually: Comprehensive financial stewardship audit
- Triggered: Review any transaction exceeding threshold amounts
- Verify reciprocity in all exchange agreements
- Monitor compensation fairness across organization

**Sacred Alignment**: All financial decisions must pass the Steward of Exchange's review question: "Is this fair exchange that serves all parties?"

---

## SCRIBE — Document Generation & Management

**Role**: Ensures all organizational documents are current, accurate, and spiritually aligned.

**Frequency**: Monthly review, annually comprehensive

**Functions**:
- Monthly: Review document currency and accuracy
- Monthly: Generate required reports and filings
- Annually: Comprehensive document audit and archival
- Maintain version control on all doctrinal documents
- Ensure all public-facing materials reflect current doctrine
- Track document dependencies and cross-references

---

## MINISTER — Worship Compliance

**Role**: Monitors worship services, ceremonies, and spiritual programming for doctrinal alignment.

**Frequency**: Weekly

**Functions**:
- Weekly: Review worship service content for doctrinal alignment
- Verify ceremony protocols follow established procedures
- Monitor guest speakers/teachers for alignment
- Ensure sacred calendar observances are maintained
- Track worship attendance and engagement patterns
- Review new spiritual programs before launch

---

## HERALD — Notifications & Escalation

**Role**: Communication channel for compliance alerts, notifications, and escalation management.

**Frequency**: Daily (always on)

**Functions**:
- Daily: Process and route compliance notifications
- Immediate: Escalate critical violations to appropriate authority
- Manage notification preferences and delivery
- Track escalation resolution timelines
- Maintain communication audit trail
- Coordinate emergency Council convening when triggered

---

## BUILDER — Entity Formation

**Role**: Manages legal entity formation, structural changes, and jurisdictional compliance.

**Frequency**: Daily monitoring, triggered on entity changes

**Functions**:
- Monitor entity registration status across jurisdictions
- Track formation document filing deadlines
- Coordinate new entity creation workflows
- Ensure structural changes pass Sovereignty Gate
- Maintain entity relationship mapping
- Review franchise formation compliance

---

## COUNSEL — Legal Intelligence

**Role**: Monitors legal landscape, regulatory changes, and legal risk exposure.

**Frequency**: Quarterly review, annually comprehensive

**Functions**:
- Quarterly: Scan for regulatory changes affecting organization
- Quarterly: Review legal risk exposure and mitigation
- Annually: Comprehensive legal compliance audit
- Monitor court cases relevant to religious organizations
- Track state-level regulatory changes
- Maintain legal precedent database

---

## SENTINEL — State Compliance

**Role**: Monitors compliance across all state jurisdictions where the organization operates.

**Frequency**: Annual per state, triggered on expansion

**Functions**:
- Annual: Review compliance in each active state
- Track state registration and filing requirements
- Monitor state-level charitable solicitation compliance
- Ensure franchise operations meet state requirements
- Coordinate multi-state compliance reporting
- Flag jurisdictional conflicts or requirements

---

## DEACON — Membership Management

**Role**: Oversees membership records, covenant compliance, and community health.

**Frequency**: Monthly

**Functions**:
- Monthly: Review membership roster accuracy
- Monthly: Track covenant renewal and compliance
- Monitor membership growth and attrition patterns
- Ensure onboarding process follows doctrine
- Track ordination candidates and progress
- Manage membership communications

---

## TITHE — Fee Calculation

**Role**: Manages tithing calculations, fee structures, and financial contribution tracking.

**Frequency**: Monthly

**Functions**:
- Monthly: Calculate and reconcile tithing records
- Monthly: Review fee structure fairness
- Track contribution patterns and trends
- Ensure tithing policies align with doctrine
- Generate contribution statements
