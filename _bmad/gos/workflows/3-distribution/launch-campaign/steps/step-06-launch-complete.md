# Step 6: Launch Complete

## STEP GOAL:
Define success criteria, finalize all sections, and produce the complete launch campaign plan.

## MANDATORY EXECUTION RULES:
- NEVER generate content without user input
- YOU ARE A FACILITATOR
- FORBIDDEN to look ahead to future steps

## Sequence of Instructions

### 1. Success Criteria
Engage the user in discussion about:
- What are the Day 1 quantitative targets (traffic, signups, revenue, press mentions)?
- What are the Week 1 quantitative targets?
- What are the Month 1 quantitative targets?
- What qualitative indicators define success (team morale, customer sentiment, brand perception)?
- How will the post-mortem be structured?
- What is the timeline for the post-mortem review?
- How will learnings be documented for future launches?

### 2. Synthesize Success Criteria
Present the success criteria back to the user. Write the **Success Criteria** section of the output document.

### 3. Final Review
Present the complete launch campaign plan for holistic review:
- Launch Overview
- Pre-Launch Checklist
- Channel Coordination Matrix
- Launch Day Timeline
- Post-Launch Monitoring Plan
- Contingency Plans
- Success Criteria

Ask the user to review for:
- Completeness of the pre-launch checklist
- Realistic timeline and resource allocation
- Clear ownership for every action item
- Alignment between channels
- Any gaps or missing elements

### 4. Save Final Document
Update `{outputFile}`:
- Set `status: complete` in frontmatter
- Update `stepsCompleted` to include `step-06-launch-complete`
- Ensure all sections are populated

### 5. Present Completion
Inform the user the launch campaign plan is complete. Suggest next steps:
- Distribute the plan to all channel owners
- Schedule the pre-launch team briefing
- Begin executing the pre-launch checklist
- Set up the launch day communication channel
- Schedule the post-launch review meeting
