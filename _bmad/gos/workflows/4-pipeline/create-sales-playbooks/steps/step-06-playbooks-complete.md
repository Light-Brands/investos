# Step 6: Sales Playbooks Complete

## STEP GOAL:
Review the complete sales playbooks document, ensure all sections are cohesive, and produce the final deliverable.

## MANDATORY EXECUTION RULES:
- NEVER generate content without user input
- YOU ARE A FACILITATOR
- FORBIDDEN to look ahead to future steps

## Sequence of Instructions

### 1. Final Review
Present the complete sales playbooks document for holistic review:
- Sales Philosophy
- Buyer Journey Map
- Discovery Framework
- Pitch Framework
- Objection Library
- Demo Script
- Competitive Battle Cards
- Closing Techniques
- Follow-Up Cadence

Ask the user to review for:
- Consistency between discovery, pitch, and demo frameworks
- Completeness of objection coverage
- Battle card accuracy and currency
- Practical usability for sales reps
- Alignment with CRM fields and processes
- Any gaps or missing elements

### 2. Sales Enablement Plan
Facilitate a discussion about:
- How will the playbooks be rolled out to the sales team?
- What training sessions are needed?
- How will reps practice and internalize the material?
- How will the playbooks be maintained and updated?
- What feedback mechanism will reps use to suggest improvements?
- Where will the playbooks live for easy access?

### 3. Save Final Document
Update `{outputFile}`:
- Set `status: complete` in frontmatter
- Update `stepsCompleted` to include `step-06-playbooks-complete`
- Ensure all sections are populated

### 4. Present Completion
Inform the user the sales playbooks are complete. Suggest next steps:
- Schedule sales team training sessions
- Set up the playbook in the sales enablement tool
- Create quick-reference cards for daily use
- Begin role-play and practice sessions
- Consider running the **create-onboarding-flow** workflow for customer onboarding design
- Consider running the **create-pipeline-architecture** workflow if pipeline is not yet designed
